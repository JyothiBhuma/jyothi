Special Programmes for Advanced Learners

The institution identifies and supports advanced learners through specialized programs designed to enhance their learning experience. These programs may include advanced courses, workshops, and additional resources tailored to meet the needs of high-achieving students.

Special Programmes for Slow Learners

Recognizing the diverse learning levels of students, the institution provides special programs for slow learners. These programs aim to offer additional support, personalized attention, and resources to help slow learners catch up with their peers and succeed academically.
